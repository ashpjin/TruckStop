WeTap is a participatory sensing campaign geared towards collecting useful data about HDTs (heavy duty trucks).

With this Android application you can tag and complete a survey about trucks that you see idling or stopped in your neighborhood.

The goal of this application is to document impacts from heavy duty trucks within the community of Boyle Heights.
